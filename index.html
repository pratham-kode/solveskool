Understanding quadrilaterals

In a quadrilateral ABCD ,

> The points A,B,C and D are called the vertices of the quadrilateral . > The line segments AB,BC,CD and DA are the sides of the quadrilateral.
> The four angles are ∠ A ∠ B ∠ C and ∠ D .
> Two sides that have a common vertex are known as adjacent sides.
> Any two sides that do not have a vertex in common are called as opposite sides .
>Two angles are said to be adjacent angles if they have a side of the quadrilateral as common
>The sum of interior angles of the quadrilateral is 360 ° .
